Informacion!

The osu!catch World Cup 2025 (CWC 2025) is a country-based osu!catch tournament hosted by the osu! team. It is the fourteenth instalment of the osu!catch World Cup.

Prizes

The osu!catch World Cup 2025 offers a $2,000 cash prize pool and limited-edition merch.
